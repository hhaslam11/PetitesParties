About us

Let us start by introducing ourselves.


My name is France and I am a wife and a mother to three children. I absolutely love throwing parties. I threw my first party at the age of 11 for my little brother and have had a passion for it ever since. Over the years my parties have gotten elaborate to the point where friends and family have suggested that I should start my own business.

My name is Shirley and I too am a wife and a mother to three children. My passion for throwing parties started after attending one of France's parties. I decided to throw my own party and fell in love with the process. I have been throwing parties for my children ever since.


We've known each other our entire lives and were very close in childhood. After reuniting in adulthood, we started talking about how much we loved to throw parties and started talking about having our own party planning business. And, so, here we are!

Petites' parties believes in celebrating children. What better way to celebrate your child than to celebrate the day that he or she was born with a theme party. From invitations to goodie bags and everything in between, our party packages help you do just that with minimal time and effort.
