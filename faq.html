Frequently Asked Questions

What is your refund policy?

When you order a theme package and are not satisfied, just return everything in it's original packaging within 30 days, and we will refund 100% of your money back. You must pay the shipping cost.

Do you require a deposit?

We do not require a deposit for the packages, however, we do require payment as soon as you order. For our carered parties, we ask for a $100 non-refundable deposit.

When is payment required?

For our pacakges, we require the full payment when you order. For our catered packages, we require a $100 non-refundable deposit at the time of booking. The rest of the payment will be accepted by the day of the party.

How far in advance must I order?

Since our parties (packages and catered) have personalized items, we require one month's notice for the packages, and 6 weeks for the catered parties. This ensures you will recieve your invitations in time to deliver them.

What do your packages come with?

Please refer to the theme package you would like to order on our website for the list of items.

What are your shipping costs?

You are responsible for you own shipping cost. Please note that these are large and heavy packages. Your shipping will be calculated based on the contents of your package and your location

Contact info

If you have any questions, inquiries, or requests; you can contact us here. please email us (Shirley and/or France) and write a short message of what you are needing, along with your name and number, and we will try to get back to you within 24 hours.

Email: contact@petitesparties.com

Payment Methods

For the security of our customers, we only accept online payments via Paypal. If you are a local resident, we also accept cash, or a cheque.
